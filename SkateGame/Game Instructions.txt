Welcome to my game. Below are the ways you can interact with it.

*Interaction:
*        up arrow or p - push/accelerate
*        down arrow or b - tail drag/break
*        right arrow - turn to the right
*        left arrow - turn to the left
*        r - reset to standing in the center facing 0 degrees.
*        s - shuvit
*        3 - 360 shuvit
*        o - ollie
*        k - kickflip
*        t - tre flip
*        right click - change board color
*        click example boards - change board color
